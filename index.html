Patient Transfer Safety Guidelines

⚠️ YELLOW RISK

Patients weighing 100 lbs or more require professional personnel and equipment for safe handling.

🚫 RED RISK

Manual handling prohibited for patients weighing 155 lbs or above. Professional equipment mandatory.

✅ NIOSH RECOMMENDATION

Currently, the National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) recommends a maximum weight lift for patient-handling tasks of 35 lbs.

In 2015, "patient transfer" was listed as one of the top ten patient safety concerns by U.S. healthcare institutions.
